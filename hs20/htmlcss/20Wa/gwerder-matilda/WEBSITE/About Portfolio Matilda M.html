About me

Matilda is a 24-year-old illustrator who lives and works in Berne, Switzerland. Art has always been a great passion of hers. Somewhat overwhelmed by the choice of lifestyle options, but looking joyfully into the future - at least most of the time. Always open for new experiences and adventures. Her favourite artists are Henri Matisse, Gustav Klimt, Frida Kahlo and many more. She likes to be inspired by all sorts of things, from a sauce stain to a visit to a museum or the hairs sticking to the wall after taking a shower.
